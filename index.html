What's up with NgTalks 2019?

Last year we held our first ever conference and it was a BLAST! This year, we took into account all the wishes and used all the experience gained from the first one to make not only the largest Angular conference in Europe, but also the most interesting and fascinating one. You read it correctly: 1300 attendees, 25+ speakers coming from all corners of the Earth, 1 track and over 25+ Angular-related talks! Geez!
